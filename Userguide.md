# User Guide: Random Thoughts

Welcome to **Random Thoughts**, an anonymous web application running on a Raspberry Pi Zero 2W. This guide explains how to use the application to share your inspirations and manage your personal history.

---

## How to Use the Application

### 1. Accessing the Application
* Open your web browser and navigate to the application's URL or the IP address of the Raspberry Pi server (e.g., `http://<your-raspberry-pi-ip>`).

### 2. Posting an Anonymous Thought
* On the main page, you will see a text input field.
* Type your thoughts, quotes, or inspirations into the field.
* Click the **[Post]** button to submit. 
* *Note: No registration or login is required. Your post is completely anonymous.*

### 3. Viewing the Ephemeral Wall
* The homepage displays the **Ephemeral Wall**, which shows posts from all users.
* This wall only displays posts written within the **last 12 hours**. 
* Posts older than 12 hours will automatically disappear from this public timeline.

### 4. Accessing Your Personal Archive
* If you want to review your past thoughts after they have vanished from the public wall, click on the **[My History]** or **[Archive]** section.
* The application uses **browser cookies** to identify your device securely without a login.
* You will be able to see a history of your own posts here.

⚠️ **Important Note on Privacy & Cookies:**
* Because your history is tracked via browser cookies, your archive is tied to the specific browser and device you used to write the posts.
* If you clear your browser cookies, use Incognito/Private mode, or switch to a different device, your personal archive history will be lost.
